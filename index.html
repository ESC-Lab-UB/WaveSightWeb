Our Products

Two Technologies, One Mission

WaveSight offers two complementary imaging platforms designed for comprehensive wound assessment, each leveraging a distinct modality to reveal what the naked eye cannot see.

Radiofrequency Imaging

WaveSight-RF

See beneath the surface

WaveSight-RF uses advanced radiofrequency sensing to image wound tissue below the skin surface, revealing subclinical information invisible to standard visual inspection.

  • Sub-surface tissue characterization
  • Non-contact, non-invasive scanning
  • Real-time depth mapping
  • Objective wound bed assessment

Optical Imaging

WaveSight-OPT

Illuminate with precision

WaveSight-OPT employs multispectral optical imaging to assess tissue oxygenation, perfusion, and bacterial presence on the wound surface, enabling data-driven clinical decisions.

  • Tissue oxygenation mapping (StO₂)
  • Bacterial fluorescence detection
  • Automated wound measurement
  • Multi-wavelength analysis

Our Technology

Science-Driven Innovation

WaveSight's proprietary imaging platforms are built on decades of research in biomedical engineering, delivering clinical-grade accuracy in a portable, easy-to-use form factor.

Radiofrequency Sensing

Proprietary RF antenna arrays generate sub-surface tissue maps by measuring dielectric properties at multiple frequencies, revealing wound bed composition and healing trajectory.

Multispectral Optics

Advanced LED arrays and hyperspectral cameras capture tissue reflectance across multiple wavelengths, enabling real-time oxygenation mapping and bacterial fluorescence detection.

AI-Powered Analytics

Machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of wound images provide automated wound measurements, tissue classification, and healing progress tracking with clinical-grade accuracy.

Point-of-Care Design

Ergonomic, handheld devices designed for bedside use. No darkroom required, no consumables needed — just power on and scan for instant, actionable wound data.

Seamless Integration

Cloud-connected platform with EHR integration capabilities. Export standardized wound reports, track healing progression over time, and share data across care teams securely.

Dual-Modality Fusion

When used together, WaveSight-RF and WaveSight-OPT data can be fused to create a comprehensive multi-layer wound profile, offering insights no single modality can achieve alone.
